ObbyStar Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 21, 2026

ObbyStar is a game made for children and families. We take privacy seriously, and we designed ObbyStar to collect as little information as possible.

Information we collect

None. ObbyStar does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. There are no user accounts, no sign-in, and no analytics or advertising services in the app.

Data stored on your device

Your game progress — such as which levels you have finished, stars, coins, gems, and any obstacle courses you build — is saved only on your own device. This information never leaves the device and is not accessible to us or anyone else.

Third parties

ObbyStar contains no third-party advertising, no third-party analytics, and no in-app purchases. We do not share any information with third parties because we do not collect any.

Children's privacy

ObbyStar is suitable for children. Because the app collects no personal information at all, it complies with children's privacy laws including COPPA.
