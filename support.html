ObbyStar Support

ObbyStar is a friendly obstacle-course (obby) game for kids: run, jump, and dash across 100 colorful levels, collect coins and gems, earn stars, and build your own courses.

How to play

  • Drag on the left side of the screen to move.
  • Tap JUMP on the right to jump. The glowing ring shows where you'll land.
  • Reach the glowing gold pad to finish a level and earn stars.
  • Earn stars to unlock more levels, or spend gems to open a locked level early.
  • Tap My Levels to build and play your own obstacle courses.

Tips for younger players

Turn on Assist mode in Settings to make levels easier and open all worlds.
